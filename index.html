About the Show

Bitcoin has
no borders.

One Love Bitcoin is a long-form interview series that travels the world to find the people building, advocating, and living on Bitcoin — from Lagos to London, El Salvador to Senegal.

Each episode goes deep: the culture, the struggle, the hope, and the conviction of people who believe Bitcoin is the most important financial tool of our time.
